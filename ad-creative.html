01

LEVÉ · Clinical Luxury

Clinical proof points.
Editorial treatment.

Product Static Clinical Annotation Meta Feed 1080×1080

Open-jar product shot on a warm gradient ground with callout annotations pointing to key clinical claims. The floating lid creates visual tension and an implied premium feel. Typography is kept editorial: serif, restrained, no visual noise. This format translates clinical credibility into a luxury aesthetic without sacrificing either.

Spec

02

FOLIA · Botanical Editorial

Natural ingredients.
Intentional composition.

Editorial Flatlay Product Static Meta Feed 1080×1080

Amber pump bottle centred on a teal ground with scattered botanical elements placed to feel studied, not styled. The asymmetric herb placement creates movement without chaos. Copy lands bottom-right: "Botanical extracts. Clinical results." The period after each line slows the reader down. Photoshop composite built for a brand that believes nature and science are not opposites.

Spec

03

PAVO · Bold Graphic

Oversized type.
Product forward.

Bold Graphic Brand Static Meta Feed 1080×1080

The tube is placed on a diagonal and the brand name bleeds behind it at scale, creating a layered composition where product and type share the same visual weight. "YOUR SKIN AMPLIFIED" runs across two lines at display size: the copy mirrors the product's promise directly. Pink ground, teal type, white product. A confident, scroll-stopping direction for a playful skincare brand that doesn't want to look like everyone else.

Creative Approach

The Brief

Demonstrate range across three static ad formats for skincare DTC brands on Meta: clinical-luxe, botanical-editorial, and bold graphic. Each execution uses the same 1:1 square canvas but arrives at a completely different visual solution.

What Makes It Work

Each ad is built to solve a specific creative problem: LEVE proves clinical claims without looking pharmaceutical. FOLIA makes natural ingredients feel intentional, not rustic. PAVO prioritises stopping power over refinement. Three different strategies, three different outputs.

Tools

Adobe Photoshop for compositing, colour grading, and final output. All three ads are original concept executions built to demonstrate what elevated static ad creative looks like for skincare brands at the DTC level.
